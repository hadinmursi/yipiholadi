Selena Gomez Wears Lace-Up Bodysuit: Street Style

Posted by Tandra Barner on Thursday, August 15, 2024

A whole lotta look, but she’s working it! Selena Gomez exited her Paris hotel on Tuesday, March 8, in quite the bold ensemble.

The “Hands to Myself” singer, 23, rocked a cleavage-baring lace-up bodysuit (it laces up the back too) and a denim patchwork micro-miniskirt. She also donned dark shades, a crisp blazer and Givenchy’s over-the-knee peep-toe boots with star-print heels (which retail for $1,395).

And as a little shout-out to the fashion show she’ll be attending on March 9, Gomez added a lunchbox-shaped Louis Vuitton monogram purse.
