Professional Career

Dustin Hurt came to a public notice after starring in Discovery Channel reality series ‘Gold Rush’. He used to formerly construct buildings and dikes in Louisiana as well as worked as a firefighter before making the television appearance.

Career Timeline:

  • From season 2 of ‘Gold Rush’, he worked at the Jim Nail Placer mine and departed from the show after season 4
