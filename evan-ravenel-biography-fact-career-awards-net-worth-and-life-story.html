Evan Ravenel (born November 24, 1989) is an American professional basketball player who plays for Akita Northern Happinets in Japan.

College career

He began his collegiate career at Boston College, and was transferred to the Ohio State Buckeyes. He helped the OSU advance to the NCAA Final Four as a college junior.

Professional career

Ravenel played for the Ryukyu Golden Kings of Okinawa in 2015-16 and won bj league championship title and he was the playoff MVP.

On January 16, 2017, he signed with the Akita Northern Happinets of B.League.

Trivia

  • He likes gyudon and yakiniku.

    Personal

    Evan and his younger sister Erin are the children of Pamela and Eugene Ravenel.
