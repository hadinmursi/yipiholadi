Al Pacino, 73, Wears Full Black Suit at the Beach, Packs on PDA With Girlfriend Lucila Sola, 33

Posted by Billy Koelling on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Well, that's one way to prevent a sunburn. Al Pacino redefined "suitable" beach wear on Thursday, April 25 — his 73rd birthday — when he hit up the Miami shore wearing a full black suit, complete with a blazer, slacks, and lace-up dress shoes.

Accompanied by his 40-years-younger girlfriend, Lucila Sola, the Godfather star looked a bit like a fish out of water as he trekked through the sand in his formal attire. Sola, 33, was fully clothed as well, but in a bohemian-chic black maxi skirt and a denim jacket.

The couple carried cups of coffee and a takeout container as they strolled along the beach together. At one point, they stopped to relax on a reclining lounge chair, with Sola cuddling up to her much-older beau and resting her head on his chest.

Later that night, Pacino — who has never married but has three children from previous relationships — wore a similar ensemble for his special One Night Only show at the Hard Rock Hotel & Casino in Hollywood, Fla. The event included a Q&A with the Oscar winner, during which he told behind-the-scenes tales from the Godfather and Scarface sets.
