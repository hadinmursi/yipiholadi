Why Cam Unfollowed Chase After 'Too Hot To Handle' Wrapped Up

Posted by Tandra Barner on Wednesday, September 4, 2024

The Netflix hit series, Too Hot To Handle returned for a second season and fans cannot get enough! Not only is the cast hotter the second time around, but the temptation to keep things PG was even more intense.

While Parisian heartthrob, Marvin Anthony took home the grand prize, it was Emily Miller and Cam Holmes who truly came out as this season's winners.

The duo has managed to keep the flame alive and well since the show has wrapped, despite Cam nearly breaking Emily's heart when fellow THTH co-star, Christina Carmela arrived. With viewers loving their reality TV love story, it appears as if the couple has bigger fish to fry with Chase DeMoor's confusing TikToks that led Cam to hit that unfollow button!

Related: 'Too Hot To Handle': Here's Where Season 2 Was Filmed

Why Cam Holmes Unfollowed Chase DeMoor?

Too Hot To Handle season two certainly didn't disappoint! When it comes to finding love in Turks and Caicos, it appears as if Emily Miller and Cam Holmes had no problem doing just that.

As Emily and Cam navigate the first few months as a couple sans the cameras, it seems as if they are already dealing with some unwanted drama.

During an exclusive interview with TheThings, Cam Holmes revealed that his friendship with fellow co-star, Chase DeMoor, is a little rocky!

When asked if there was any behind-the-scenes drama following the end of season two, Cam shared that Chase had been making some rather confusing claims on TikTok regarding his time on Too Hot To Handle.

Chase went on to allude to having had a fling with Emily during the filming of the show, despite none of that transpiring on-screen, and Cam and Emily were just as confused as the rest of us.

"Chase, for some reason started making up these strange TikToks about the fact that he and Em were a thing," Cam said. "We sat there confused!" Same, Cam, same!

"So confused," Emily agreed. "This was before we had announced to the world that we were still together, and he was trying to get the clout that me and Chase were together and it was so bizarre," she said.

Well, this didn't sit well with Cam, who made it clear he wasn't about any type of that nonsense, prompting him to unfollow Chase on social media.

"Cam unfollowed Chase on Instagram," Emily shared. "Yeah, I unfollowed him. It annoyed me, what are you trying to do?" he told TheThings.

Despite Chase's puzzling videos, it didn't stand a chance at phasing either Cam or Emily, who fans have now shipped as "Camily".

Cam & Emily's 'Too Hot To Handle' Love Story

The duo first locked lips at the very beginning of the season, costing the group a lot of money, however, when things are, in fact, too hot to handle, the rules are then meant to be broken!

The South Wales dreamboat quickly found himself falling for Emily, that is until producers surprised the house with two new cast members. Upon Christina Carmela's arrival, the star requested a private date with Cam, getting him in quite a lot of trouble.

Luckily for Holmes, he and Emily managed to work through their relationships first major speed bump, and fans are happy they did!

Emily and Cam have not only remained a couple following the season wrap-up, but the two have officially moved in together!

While they lived together during their time filming the show, the duo is learning more and more about one another as they embark on their new relationship, however, with every new relationship, comes a few quarrels.

"We bicker a lot, but it's over such petty things!" Emily told TheThings. "He leaves the lights on, and drives like a granny" Em shared before Cam revealed Emily was actually the bad driver.

As the two laughed it off, reminding us all why we fell in love with them in the first place, they aren't the only couple to have made it!

In addition to these two lovebirds' fairytale romance, it appears as if fellow co-stars, Melinda Melrose and Peter Vigilante have also rekindled their romance post THTH, making it clear that love is always in the air!

Speaking of Melinda, during an Instagram live with Em and Cam, Melinda revealed that she too unfollowed Chase! While she didn't want to get into it after Emily asked if it was because of the "Marvin thing", it's become clear that somethings up with Chase DeMoor and the rest of the cast.
