Not Every Frasier Cast Member Was Worth A Fortune When They Died

Posted by Billy Koelling on Tuesday, September 3, 2024

The timeless charm and sophisticated, cozy set of Frasier was where witty banter and fun were always in great supply. But what about the real lives of the beloved cast members? Did the spotlight of success shine equally on all its stars? Beyond the studio's bright lights, the story isn’t always as prosperous.

While some of the stars of this iconic sitcom amassed fortunes that matched their on-screen characters, others had surprisingly modest net worths when they eventually died. Who among the Frasier cast turned their fame into a financial fairy tale, and who faced a more humble reality?

8 How Much Was Rosemary Murphy Worth When She Died?

Net Worth: Estimated at $100,000 - $1 million

  • Role on Frasier: Carol Larkin

According to Idol Net Worth, Rosemary Murphy who made only two appearances as Carol Larkin, Niles' neighbor, had an estimated net worth ranging from $100,000 to $1 million at the time of her death in 2014. Her acting career was extensive and varied, spanning stage, film, and television. She was a three-time Tony Award nominee for her stage work and won an Emmy Award in 1976 for her performance in the television movie Eleanor and Franklin, where she portrayed Sara Delano Roosevelt​​​.

7 How Much Was Marsha Kramer's Net Worth At The Time Of Her Death?

Net Worth: Estimated at $1million - $5 million

  • Role on Frasier: Tooty

Marsha Kramer, who played Tooty the Story Lady, had an estimated net worth between $1 million and $5 million at the time of her death in January 2020,​​ as per Celebs Age Wiki. Her career was marked by notable achievements in various facets of the performing arts. Her early career was predominantly in theater, where she shone on Broadway in roles such as Wendy in the 1979 revival of Peter Pan. Her other significant theatrical roles included Agnes Gooch in Mame and Amy in Company alongside Carol Burnett​.

6 John LaMotta's Net Worth When He Died

Net Worth: Estimated at $1 million - $5 million

  • Role on Frasier: Duke

According to Celebs Age Wiki, John LaMotta, who portrayed Duke, Marty Crane's favorite bartender in seasons one and two of the sitcom, had an estimated net worth ranging between $1 million and $5 million at the time of his death in 2014, at the age of 75​​. LaMotta's acting career was his primary source of income.

5 Alice Playten's Net Worth When She Died

Net Worth: Estimated at $1.5 million

  • Role on Frasier: Bonnie

According to Wikilogy, Alice Playten, who played Marty's girlfriend Bonnie, was estimated to have a net worth of $1.5 million at the time of her death in 2011. Playten's remarkable career began in her hometown of New York City, where she debuted in the original production of Gypsy, playing Baby Louise. She quickly became known for her unique talents, later drawing comparisons to the legendary Ethel Merman​​. Playten's breakthrough came in 1967 with the musical Henry, Sweet Henry, which earned her a Tony Award nomination and a Theatre World Award.

4 How Much Was Bob Hoskins Worth When He Died?

Net Worth: Estimated at $4 million

  • Role on Frasier: Coach Fuller

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Bob Hoskins, who appeared as Coach Fuller, was a celebrated British A-list actor and film director and was reported to have a net worth of between $4 million at the time of his death in 2014​. His diverse career in the entertainment industry spanned several decades, marked by significant achievements and contributions to film and theatre.

3 How Much Was Robert Loggia Worth When He Died?

Net Worth: Estimated at $10 million

  • Role on Frasier: Stefano

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Robert Loggia had an estimated net worth of $10 million at the time of his death in 2015​​. He was a guest star in the show and played Stefano, a restaurant owner. Loggia's career spanned over six decades, during which he appeared in numerous film and television projects. He made a breakthrough in Hollywood with his portrayal of Elfego Baca in the Walt Disney TV series The Nine Lives of Elfego Baca.

2 How Much Was Rene Auberjonois Worth When He Died?

Net Worth: Estimated at $10 million

  • Role on Frasier: Dr William Tewksbury

According to Investor Times, René Auberjonois, who played Dr. William Tewksbury, had an estimated net worth of $10 million at the time of his death in 2019. He embarked on an illustrious career that spanned over five decades, earning him widespread acclaim and financial success. His journey to fame began on the stage, where he achieved early prominence as a Tony Award-winning actor for his role in the musical Coco in 1970. Auberjonois’s stage career was marked by diverse roles and consistent acclaim, earning him three more Tony nominations for his performances in The Good Doctor (1973), Big River (1985), and City of Angels (1989), along with a Drama Desk Award for Big River.

1 John Mahoney's Impressive Net Worth When He Died

Net Worth: Estimated at $16 million

  • Role on Frasier: Martin Crane

According to Celebrity Net Worth, John Mahoney, who portrayed Martin Crane in Frasier, had a net worth estimated at $16 million at the time of his death in 2018​. His remarkable net worth was largely attributed to his successful acting career, particularly his iconic role on the long-running sitcom Frasier from 1993 to 2004​​.
