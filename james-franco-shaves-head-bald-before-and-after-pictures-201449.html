James Franco Shaves Head, Goes Bald: See Before-and-After Pictures!

Posted by Patria Henriques on Saturday, August 24, 2024

That’s a bold choice! James Franco got a drastic makeover on Wednesday, Sept. 3, when he shaved off all of his hair, going completely bald. The Palo Alto actor, 36, debuted the shocking transformation on Instagram to his 2.7 million followers. 

“Say Goodbye to this hair… You'll see. Tomorrow,” he wrote along with a head shot before chopping off his brunette locks. 

Franco then posted two identical mirror selfies, one with hair and one without.
