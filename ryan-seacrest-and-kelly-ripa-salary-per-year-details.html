Is Ryan Seacrest Making More Than Co-Host Kelly Ripa Per Year?

Posted by Billy Koelling on Saturday, August 31, 2024

Ryan John Seacrest has built a fortune as a media personality and producer. He has become a recognizable face of multiple media platforms, including American Idol, American Top 40, and On Air with Ryan Seacrest.

In 2006, Seacrest became co-host and executive producer of Dick Clark's New Year's Rockin' Eve. He remained host and executive producer of the annual show broadcast from Times Square in New York City following Dick Clark's death in 2012.

Seacrest received Emmy Award nominations for American Idol from 2004 to 2013, and again in 2016. He went on to win an Emmy for producing Jamie Oliver's Food Revolution. Seacrest became a permanent fixture of the E! Network and earned a lucrative contract as the creator and executive producer on Keeping Up With The Kardashians.

Seacrest began co-hosting Live with...alongside Kelly Ripa on May 1, 2017. It came after Michael Strahan and ABC announced that he was leaving the show to join ABC's Good Morning America full-time. On May 1, 2018, Ripa and Seacrest celebrated their first anniversary with a look back at the best moments of the past year. They did the same thing the following year to celebrate their second anniversary. The pair have been hosting the show together for five years.

Ryan Seacrest CV speaks for himself, however Kelly Ripa has been working on Live with... for over 20 years. So who earns more?

Kelly Ripa Boasts An Estimated Net Worth Of $120 Million

Kelly Maria Ripa is known for her acting roles as well as her presenting talents. Ripa played Hayley Vaughan on the ABC daytime soap opera All My Children. She co-starred alongside her future husband Mark Consuelos. In 1996, they eloped with the couple now having three children: Michael Joseph (born June 2, 1997), Lola Grace (born June 16, 2001), and Joaquin Antonio (born February 24, 2003). Since 2001, Ripa has been the co-host of the syndicated morning talk show Live! with Kelly and Ryan in various formats.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, she boasts an estimated net worth of $120 million. Forbes reported in 2018 that Kelly earns more than $20 million a year for hosting Live With Kelly and Ryan. Her salary from the show has significantly increased over the years. For instance, back in 2004, it was revealed that the actress/host signed a $40 million contract extension that would see her remain on Live! for another five years. But then, her $8 million a year back then certainly pales in comparison to the salary that she earns now.

Ryan Seacrest Has A Net Worth Of $450 Million, Along With A Larger Per Year Salary

After hosting American Idol for a number of years, Ryan Seacrest secured a whopping $15 million per season, which he earned until the show ended its 14-year run in 2016. When he returned for the show’s reboot in 2018, Ryan began reportedly banking somewhere between $10 and $15 million. In 2006, Seacrest signed his first TV deal with E! and launched Ryan Seacrest Productions. Just six years later, Bain Capital invested $300 million in the firm, and that same year, five years after that, RSP signed a deal with ABC to produce scripted content.

Seacrest has also signed on to support large brands and specific products, appearing in commercials and attending in-person events on their behalf. He has done so for companies such as Coca-Cola and Procter & Gamble. Forbes reported that Seacrest was earning $5 million from endorsement deals.

In 2017, when Seacrest landed the seat next to Kelly Ripa his yearly total earnings rose to $75 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. Between June 2017 and June 2018, Ryan Seacrest earned $74 million. His estimated net worth is reportedly an eye watering $450 million which is triple the earnings of his co-star Kelly Ripa.

Kelly Ripa And Ryan Seacrest Are Incredibly Close

Live With Kelly and Ryan stars Kelly Ripa and Ryan Seacrest are both beloved cohosts with massive net worths.

The pair clearly have a great relationship and awesome chemistry that makes fans love them.

Seacrest and Ripa spend a lot of their time together on the show but also have an extremely close friendship on a personal level. Ripa dished on their tight bond to People, revealing that Seacrest is also close her husband, Mark Consuelos, and their three kids, Michael, Lola and Joaquin.

“He does intel with my kids,” Kelly explained to People in February 2020. “He doesn’t betray their confidences, but he will give me a heads up if there’s something going on because they don’t think of him as an adult … he’s one of them.”

Ripa even says Seacrest helps round her home. “He will do things for me if I’m traveling,” Kelly dished. “I can ask him to go inside my house … and go bring my retainer out of the medicine cabinet and bring it to the remote.”
