10 'Real Housewives' Kids Who Always Steal The Show

Posted by Billy Koelling on Monday, August 12, 2024

When it comes to Bravo's Real Housewives, the ladies and sometimes their husbands certainly know how to bring the drama. Whether you're a fan of Jersey, New York, or the women of Dallas, there are a plethora of iconic moments to go around.

While our fav housewives have delivered, many of their kids have also brought it, in ways that have often stolen the show! Considering many Housewives stars have had kids during their time on the show, there are many personalities to choose from.

Now, with Andy Cohen's ready for a first-time-ever Bravo kids special on WWHL, we're taking a stroll down memory lane and talking about some of the most legendary Housewives kids to grace our screens!

10 Gia Giudice

Gia Giudice was first introduced to Bravo fans back in 2009 when her mother, Teresa Giudice became an official RHONJ cast member. Gia was only 8 at the time, and despite being so young, she was always so mature!

Related: ‘RHONJ’: Fans Root On Gia Giudice For Her Maturity

One show-stopping moment was the song that she wrote for her mom and uncle, Joe Gorga, at her sister's birthday party. "Waking up in the morning, thinking about so many things" the song starts, while Gia stole the show then, she's back at it again as the song has now gone viral on TikTok!

9 Milania Giudice

The Giudice's are certainly a family that is full of BIG personalities! Much like her older sister, Gia, Milania Giudice has been a fan favorite on the Real Housewives of New Jersey since the very beginning.

At only 3 years old, Milania had viewers at home laughing, whether it was from her posing, or claiming to be "fabulous", Milania always brought the glitz and glam. As she got older, her relationship with her father, Joe Giudice, was one for the books!

8 Frankie Catania

The Real Housewives of New Jersey is easily the sole franchise that puts such a focus on family life and the housewives' kids. After Dolores Catania joined the show in season 7, she brought along her incredibly stunning family, including her son Frankie Catania.

The star might not make too many appearances on the show, but when he does, he steals the scene, every single time, especially when it came to his prom scene with fellow RHONJ kid, Gia Giudice.

7 Brooks Marks

Brooks Marks made his very first appearance on the most recent Bravo addition, the Real Housewives of Salt Lake City. The series first aired back in November 2020 and instantly became a fan favorite. Brooks, who is the son of cast member, Meredith Marks, took fans on an adventure when it came to his tracksuit line.

Related: 'RHOBH' Brandi Glanville's Kids Seem All Grown Up

In addition to his creative ways, Marks also had fans laughing over his snarky comments and wise one-liners that made him quite the Housewives addition.

6 Brielle Biermann

Brielle Biermann is the eldest daughter of Real Housewives of Atlanta star, Kim Zolciak. The duo are the best of friends and share quite a special relationship. While Brielle didn't make too much of a splash in past seasons, she definitely came in hot back in 2018 when she came for NeNe Leakes.

Brielle posted a video from inside NeNe's bathroom where a roach was crawling on the floor. Brielle became a pertinent part of the storyline, which was a series first when it came to the RHOA kids.

5 Briana Culberson

Briana Culberson is none other than the daughter of Real Housewives of Orange County legend, Vicki Gunvalson. Briana was 18 when RHOC first aired, so fans were able to witness her first car, graduation, and first love all on camera.

While she always brought something to the show, Briana really made her voice heard during her mother's relationship with ex-fiancé, Brooks Ayers, especially when it came to the fake cancer storyline!

4 Christopher Manzo

Christopher Manzo was without a doubt a fan favorite RHONJ kid! Chris was the second child to former cast member, Caroline Manzo. Chris knew how to make just about everyone laugh, both on and off-screen.

Related: The Kids of The Real Housewives: Where Are They Now?

The star was mostly known for wanting to open up a strip club, which his parents weren't too fond of. Luckily for Chris, his antics led him to be a partner at none other than blk water.

3 Olivia Aydin

It appears as if there is something running in the water in New Jersey because the kids on RHONJ certainly know how to leave a lasting impression. Although RHONJ cast member, Jennifer Aydin may only be on her third season, her youngest daughter, Olivia Aydin, has become a fan-favorite! With her iconic one-liners, sass, and impressions, Olivia has stolen the hearts of viewers across the country, and she isn't stopping anytime soon.

2 Jagger Kemsley

Dorit Kemsley has become a staple figure amongst the cast of the Real Housewives of Beverly Hills. While she may have only come in halfway through, the star has definitely made her mark, and her adorable son, Jagger, has certainly helped with that.

Dorit has allowed cameras into her personal life, which has led many viewers to fall in love with her son, Jagger. While he may have had his struggles with his speech at first, Jagger is now talking more than ever, and RHOBH fans got a peek of just that during the last two seasons, and they cannot get enough!

1 Gigi Hadid

Before Gigi Hadid found herself on the runways in Paris, Milan, and New York City, she was a Bravo kid on none other than The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills. Hadid and younger siblings, Bella and Anwar are the kids of former RHOBH star, Yolanda Hadid.

While they may not have been on the show all the time, the few appearances they had focused much on Lyme disease, and considering Gigi's status, the model used her platform to spread awareness regarding the disease, which fans could not have been happier about.
