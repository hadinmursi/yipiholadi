Elimination Chamber Qualifying Match, Imperium, More Set For 2/12 WWE RAW

Posted by Tandra Barner on Sunday, August 11, 2024

The February 12 episode of WWE RAW is set to feature another Elimination Chamber qualifying match, as well as a heated six-man tag team bout.

As confirmed during the February 5 episode of RAW, Liv Morgan will face Zoey Stark in an Elimination Chamber qualifying match. The winner will join Becky Lynch, who became the first woman to clinch a spot in the match on Monday. She defeated Shayna Baszler in a qualifying match.

Elsewhere in the show, R-Truth and JD McDonagh will face off, with Truth once again having to face off against a member of The Judgment Day as he tries to prove his place in the group. Imperium will also look to send a message to the trio of New Day and Jey Uso. Uso confronted Gunther, the reigning WWE Intercontinental Champion, on Monday.

As of this writing, the announced card for the February 12 episode of WWE RAW can be seen below:

  • : Liv Morgan vs. Zoey Stark
  • R-Truth vs. JD McDonagh
  • Imperium (Gunther, Ludwig Kaiser & Giovanni Vinci) vs. Jey Uso & the New Day (Kofi Kingston & Xavier Woods)

WrestleZone will have coverage of WWE RAW as it airs on Monday, February 12.
