'90 Day Fiance' Baby Bumps: Reality Stars' Pregnancy Pics

Posted by Tandra Barner on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

Many 90 Day Fiancé personalities have shown off their baby bumps during pregnancy.

Yara Zaya, who is married to Jovi Dufren, exclusively spoke to Us Weekly in January 2021 about the fear she felt taking a pregnancy test on her own ahead of their daughter Myla’s arrival.

“I was feeling so much scared because as a woman who had [a] miscarriage, to go again [and get] pregnant would be, like … something crazy,” Yara explained to Us. “Especially because when I [had my] last miscarriage, my doctor was telling me that I will never have kids maybe. There is a big possibility.”

The couple kept their pregnancy under wraps but shared their little one’s milestones with Us three months later. “When I put her on her belly, she turns,” she gushed in April 2021. “She started turning so quickly. She’s a pretty strong baby.”
