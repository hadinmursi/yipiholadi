Gabby Windey Job

Windey is a professional ICU nurse at the UCHealth University of Colorado Hospital. She worked for seven years as a registered nurse in the medical ICU at the hospital. Later, she was a cheerleader for NFL’s Denver Broncos for five years. When the pandemic hit in 2020, Windey resumed her job as a nurse. She co-won Pop Warner Humanitarian Award for her service on the frontline of the pandemic.
