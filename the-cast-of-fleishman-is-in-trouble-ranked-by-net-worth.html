The Cast Of Fleishman Is In Trouble Ranked By Net Worth

Posted by Patria Henriques on Wednesday, August 28, 2024

Fleishman is in Trouble is a TV drama series about a recently divorced man in his 40s who uses dating apps for the first time. As Fleishman starts living his life again, dating, and gets a promotion, his ex-wife Rachel disappears without warning, leaving him to care for their kids. He soon realizes that he'll never be able to solve what happened to Rachel until he faces what happened to their marriage to begin with. Fleishman is in Trouble premiered on FX on Hulu in November 2022 and was created by Taffy Brodesser-Akner based on the novel she published in 2019 of the same name.

The miniseries stars Jesse Eisenberg, Lizzy Caplan, Claire Danes, Maxim Jasper Swinton, Meara Mahoney Gross, and Adam Brody, among others. The miniseries received nominations for the 80th Golden Globe Awards and 20th Critics’ Choice Awards for Best Supporting Actress in a Limited Series for Danes. The cast is ranked below by net worth, including details about how they earned their wealth, their roles in the miniseries and where else they have appeared aside from Fleishman is in Trouble. From Jesse Eisenberg, to Lizzy Caplan, Adam Brody, and more, here's the cast of Fleishman is in Trouble, ranked by net worth.

10 Meara Mahoney Gross, Estimated Net Worth Of $100-300k

Meara Mahoney Gross appears in Fleishman is in Trouble as Hannah, the oldest child of Toby and Rachel who gets caught in the middle of their divorce and new lives. Gross has accumulated an estimated net worth of $100-300k. She has previously appeared inThe True Ones, Christmas on Ice, Godmother, The Tender Bar, Don’t Look Up, and more. Her regional and local stage credits include performances in Once Upon a Mattress, Narnia: The Musical, Seussical the Musical and an adaptation of Twelfth Night. Aside from acting, Gross is also an accomplished vocalist, plays guitar, ukulele and piano, and studies improv with The Groundlings.

9 Maxim Jasper Swinton, Estimated Net Worth Of $1 Million

Maxim Jasper Swinton appears in Fleishman is in Trouble as Solly, Fleishman’s nine-year-old son and the youngest of his children shared with Rachel, who is sweet and supportive of him. Swinton has accumulated a net worth of approximately $1 million. He is best known for his award-winning work as a stand-up comedian, actor, singer, and writer. For his stand-up performances, he was awarded from a major international comedy festival. He has appeared in starring and supporting roles in numerous TV, film, theater, and music videos. Swinton has also performed in Matthew’s Monsters, The Blacklist, Keylight, Blindspot, Safe and Sound, T11 Incomplete, Halston, Adopted, and Raymond and Ray, among others.

8 Juani Feliz, Estimated Net Worth Of $1 Million

Juani Feliz appears in Fleishman is in Trouble as Alejandra Lopez, a playwright who stars on Broadway and is also one of Rachel's theater clients. Feliz has amassed an estimated net worth of $1 million. She has appeared in starring and supporting roles in The Purge: Election Year, Blue Bloods, Power, Shades of Blue, One Dollar, Canal Street, and more. In 2017, she was one of 12 actors selected for the ABC Discovers Talent Showcase. In 2019, she was a series regular on ABC’s Until The Wedding, and performed in Quiet In My Town. She also appears in Below the Belt, Harlem, DMZ, and will next appear in Civil War.

7 Joy Suprano, Estimated Net Worth Of $1-5 Million

Joy Suprano appears in Fleishman is in Trouble as Cyndi Leffer, the mother of Hannah's friend, who is a friend of Rachel's, and represents the rich, superficial people she admires. Suprano has amassed an estimated net worth of approximately $1-5 million. She is best known for her starring role in the Apple+ TV show Best Foot Forward. She appeared in Hightown, Almost Family, Ray Donovan, Law & Order: CI, How To Make It In America, Law & Order: SVU, and Law & Order. Her work also includes the films Lonely Boy, A Little Help, We Are The Hartmans, and a performance in the theatrical role of Jan-The-Sly in Yeast Nation, A Triumph Of Life.

6 Michael Gaston, Estimated Net Worth Of $5 Million

Michael Gaston appears in Fleishman is in Trouble as Dr. Bartuck, Fleishman’s overbearing boss at the hospital with his own fast path to the top. Gaston has amassed an estimated net worth of $5 million. He is best known for W, Bridge of Spies, Sudden Death, Ransom, Cop Land, Thirteen Days, The Crucible, Double Jeopardy, High Crimes, Sugar and Body of Lies, Bless the Child, and more. His television work includes The Sopranos, The West Wing, Homicide: Life on the Street, Law & Order, Law & Order: Special Victims Unit, Fringe, The Practice, and 24. He has also performed on Broadway in A Day in the Death of Joe Egg, and off-Broadway in Henry V, Landscape of the Body, and Fifty Ways.

5 Josh Stamberg, Estimated Net Worth Of $5 Million

Josh Stamberg appears in Fleishman Is in Trouble as Sam Rothberg. Stamberg has amassed an estimated net worth of $5 million. His television work includes Parenthood, The Affair, Nashville, The Loudest Voice, Wanda Vision, The Rookie, Minx, Magnum P.I., The Time Traveler's Wife, and more. His most recent filmography includes Afternoon Delight, Saving Lincoln, Dark Skies, Day Out of Days, Havenhurst, Home Again, The Etruscan Smile, and Operation Varsity Blues: The College Admissions Scandal. He also appears in Fracture, J. Edgar, Legion, Drop Dead Diva, Castle, The Whole Truth, Perception, and Unforgettable, among others.

4 Lizzy Caplan, Estimated Net Worth Of $5 Million

Lizzy Caplan co-stars in Fleishman is in Trouble as Libby, one of Fleishman's best friends, who struggles with her own life crisis, and serves as the narrator of the story. Caplan has amassed an estimated net worth of $5 million. She began acting on the television series Freaks and Geeks, and then garnered more popularity with Mean Girls, Cloverfield, Related, The Class, and Party Down. From 2013 to 2016, Caplan portrayed Virginia E. Johnson on Showtime’s Masters of Sex, earning a nomination for Outstanding Lead Actress in a Drama Series at the Primetime Emmy Awards. She also performed in Castle Rock, Hot Tub Time Machine, 127 Hours, Save the Date, Bachelorette, The Interview, Now You See Me 2, Allied, and Extinction.

3 Jesse Eisenberg, Estimated Net Worth Of $12 Million

Jesse Eisenberg stars in Fleishman is in Trouble as Dr. Toby Fleishman, a hepatologist in his 40s, who divorced his wife of 15 years and struggles to navigate his life. Eisenberg has amassed an estimated net worth of $12 million. He is best known for starring in The Social Network, Adventureland, Zombieland, Rio, To Rome with Love, Café Society, Now You See Me, The Double, Rio 2, The End of the Tour, Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice, Justice League, The Art of Self-Defense, and more.He also performed in Get Real, Roger Dodger, The Emperor's Club, The Village, The Squid and the Whale, and The Education of Charlie Banks.

2 Adam Brody, Estimated Net Worth Of $16 Million

Adam Brody appears in Fleishman is in Trouble as Seth, one of Fleishman's best friends who is the same age as him but still living his life as a bachelor. Brody has amassed an estimated net worth of $16 million. His breakthrough role was starring on the television series The O.C. from 2003 to 2007, followed by performances in Mr. & Mrs. Smith, Thank You for Smoking, In the Land of Women, and Jennifer's Body. Brody appears in Seeking a Friend for the End of the World, Sleeping with Other People, Lovelace, and was the star and producer of StartUp. He has also appeared in Shazam!, Ready or Not, Promising Young Woman, The Kid Detective, and will next appear in the 2023 sequel of Shazam!.

1 Claire Danes, Estimated Net Worth Of $35 Million

Claire Danes performs in Fleishman is in Trouble as Rachel, the ex-wife of Fleishman who disappears without warning and leaves their children in his care while experiencing a nervous breakdown. Danes has amassed an estimated net worth of $35 million. She first garnered popularity starring in My So-Called Life, which earned a Golden Globe Award for Best Actress and nomination for a Primetime Emmy Award. She made her film debut with Little Women, and gained more recognition for starring in Romeo + Juliet alongside Leonardo DiCapprio. Danes also performs in The Rainmaker, The Hours, Terminator 3: Rise of the Machines, Shopgirl, Stardust, the Off-Broadway production of The Vagina Monologues, the Broadway revival of Pygmalion, and more.
