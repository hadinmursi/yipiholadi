Gabriela Berlingeri &amp; Bad Bunny Make Up A Sweet Couple With Snaps and Pictures From Their Daily Live

Posted by Kelle Repass on Thursday, September 5, 2024

Gabriela Berlingeri is the girlfriend of famous Puerto Rican rapper Bad Bunny with whom she has been in a relationship for a long time. Continue reading the article to know more.

Gabriela Berlingeri is a famous social media star and model who has gained a massive fan following on her Instagram handle, where she is registered as @gabrielaberlingeri. Besides that, Berlingeri got more into media prominence after she began her relationship with Puerto Rican rapper Bad Bunny.

The couple has lately gained people’s attention after they were spotted in Miami over the weekend following a whirlwind week of public appearances and high-profile events. They were snapped on the beach. According to Daily Mail, Berlingeri and Bunny relaxed in a VIP cabana of the Faena Miami Beach.

Furthermore, the rapper Bunny recently released his fifth studio album, Un Verano Sin Ti, or A Summer Without You, which is taking over Spotify at the moment. So, fans are also eager to know more about his personal life.

Wikipedia – Who Is Gabriela Berlingeri?

Gabriela Berlingeri is the girlfriend of Bad Bunny. Apart from being known as the celebrity girlfriend, Berlingeri is also noted for her own work as she is a model and jewelry designer. The jewelry designer runs her own selling company named DiciembreVeintinueve.

From her shop, Berlingeri delivers various fashionable stuff, including Necklaces, Bracelets, Rings, and Earrings. All of them are handmade, so it is loved by many people. Besides that, she has also been an important part of Bad Bunny as she has helped him in various steps of his career.

Not to mention, she assisted Bunny in recording the 2018 song Te Guste by providing scratch vocals for Jennifer Lopez’s portions of the song. Furthermore, Berlingeri also became the first Latina to shoot the cover of Rolling Stone when she snapped the rapper for the magazine’s May 2020 cover.

Bad Bunny Girlfriend’s Real Name and Age Revealed

Bad Bunny girlfriend’s real name is Gabriela Berlingeri, whose current age is 28 years. She was born on December 29, 1993, in Puerto Rico. So, she holds Puerto Rican nationality. Meanwhile, the rapper Bunny was born Benito Antonio Martínez Ocasio on March 10, 1994, making his age 28 years. Moreover, the pair have an age gap of nine months.

Furthermore, Gabriela has a strong presence on Instagram, gaining massive followers. According to Famous Birthdays, she launched her account in September 2018, and now her profile has been verified.

Are Bad Bunny and Gabriela Berlingeri Engaged?

Rapper Bad Bunny and his girlfriend, Gabriela Berlingeri, are not engaged, but they have been dating each other for quite a while now. The couple met for the first time in 2017 at a restaurant while dining with Bunny’s family, and the two started dating soon after.
