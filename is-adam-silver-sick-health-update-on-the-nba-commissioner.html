Is Adam Silver Sick? Health Update On The NBA Commissioner

Posted by Billy Koelling on Friday, August 23, 2024

Is Adam Silver Sick? What happened to him? Tune in to find out.

Adam Silver is a lawyer and sports executive. He is currently working as the commissioner of the National Basketball Association. In 1992, he joined the NBA and has worked in different positions within the league.

Prior to working as, the commissioner, Silver also served as the chief operating officer of the NBA. In 2014, he was announced as the new commissioner after David Stern retired.

Let us learn about Adam Silver in detail.

Is Adam Silver Sick? Health Update

No news about Adam Silver being sick has surfaced on the internet yet. Since he has not talked much about his health on a public platform, not much can be said about it.

We will have to wait for Silver to shed light on this topic.

You can, however, follow him on his social media accounts to stay updated about his life. Silver is active on Instagram as @adamsilvernba, where he has already garnered 467k followers.

Is Adam Silver Jewish?

Yes, Adam Silver is Jewish. He was born into a Jewish-American family.

Just like Adam, his father, Edward Silver was also a lawyer. Edward specialized in labor law and was a senior partner at the law firm Proskauer Rose.

Raised in Rye, New York, Adam graduated from Rye High School in 1980. For his undergraduate studies, Silver attended Duke University. He has a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Political Science. After graduating from Duke in 1982, he worked as a legislative aide to Les AuCoin.

Moving on, he has a J.D. degree from the University of Chicago Law School. He then worked as a law clerk for Judge Kimba Wood.  Later, he served as an associate in Cravath, Swaine & Moore, a law firm.

Details To Know About Adam Silver

Adam Silver was born in 1962 in Rye, New York, and is of American Nationality. His age is 59 years old and celebrates his birthday on April 25 each passing year.

Similarly, Adam is a married man. He got married to Maggie Silver in 2015, and has two children together; both daughters. Their oldest daughter was born in April 2017 while the youngest one was born in May 2020.

Talking about his achievements, Adam was recognized as the Sports Illustrated Executive of the Year in 2014. He has also been named as one of Fortune’s 50 Greatest Leaders and Time’s 100 Most Influential People.
