Alicia Silverstone News - Us Weekly

Posted by Patria Henriques on Thursday, September 5, 2024

Name:Alicia Silverstone

Hometown:San Francisco, CA

Relationship:Separated

Birthday:October 4, 1976

About

Nineties teen queen Alicia Silverstone shined in the 1995 hit comedy Clueless, which led to the creation of her own production company, First Kiss Productions. But the actress didn’t always hit it so big.

She began her career in 1988 with a guest appearance on The Wonder Years as a dream girl, and landed her first big screen role in 1993’s drama The Crush. Since then, the California girl mostly sticks to the small screen with made-for-TV movies and dating sitcoms.

In June 2005, the vegan star married longtime beau Christopher Jarecki, lead singer of rock band Underdogs, after seeing him for nearly eight years. The couple welcomed son son Bear Blu on May 5, 2011.

Silverstone has a popular blog, “The Kind Life,” which discusses vegan and vegetarian food as well as environmental issues.
