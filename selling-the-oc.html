Selling the OC News - Us Weekly

Posted by Patria Henriques on Friday, August 16, 2024

Network:Netflix

Selling the OC is a spinoff of the popular Netflix reality series Selling Sunset, which follows the agents of the Oppenheim Group, a high-end real estate firm, as they navigate their personal and professional lives.

The reality show stars the cofounder of Oppenheim Group Jason Oppenheim as well as his employees Alex Hall, Alexandra Jarvis, Alexandra Rose, Brandi Marshall, Gio Helou, Kayla Cardona, Polly Brindle, Austin Victoria and Tyler Stanaland.

Selling the OC premiered on Netflix in 2022. It was renewed for a second and third season in January 2023.
