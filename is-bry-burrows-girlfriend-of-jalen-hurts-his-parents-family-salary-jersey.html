Is Bry Burrows, Girlfriend of Jalen Hurts? His Parents, Family, Salary &amp; Jersey

Posted by Tandra Barner on Friday, September 6, 2024

Jalen Hurts  is a quarterback in the NFL for the Philadelphia Eagles. He spent his first three seasons of collegiate football at Alabama, where he was a member of the squad that won the 2018 College Football Playoff National Championship, before transferring to Oklahoma for his final year of eligibility.

Hurts was drafted in the second round of the 2020 NFL Draft by the Eagles and became their starting quarterback near the end of his first season. Hurts has no doubt set his sights on making a name for himself in the NFL, especially after a successful college career that generated high expectations.

Jalen’s Salary

The Philadelphia Eagles signed Jalen Hurts to a four-year, $6,025,171 contract that included a $1,941,944 signing fee, $2,825,815 in guaranteed money, and a $1,506,293 average annual salary. Hurts will make $1,082,744 in basic pay and $75,000 in workout bonus in 2022, while generating a $1,643,230 cap hit and a $970,972 fixed amount. This would pay him $36 million per season, putting him ahead of Carson Wentz, Jared Goff, and Kirk Cousins.
