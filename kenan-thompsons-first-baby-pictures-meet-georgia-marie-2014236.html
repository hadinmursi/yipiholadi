Kenan Thompson's First Baby Pictures: Meet Georgia Marie

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

Precious pictures! Kenan Thompson‘s wife Christina Evangeline shared two adorable snaps of their two-day-old baby daughter via Instagram on Sunday, June 22.

The Saturday Night Live star welcomed baby Georgia Marie into the world on Friday, June 20, as Us Weekly previously reported. Despite being exhausted by their new parenting schedule, proud mom Evangeline managed to find the time to post a couple of pictures of their baby daughter on the social networking site.

One picture shows devoted dad Thompson staring lovingly into his newborn daughters crib, captioned “while you were sleeping…” In another adorable photograph, model Evangeline looks radiant, commenting, “We’re just really sleepy.”

Thompson, 36, first revealed his wife’s pregnancy during an appearance on Late Night With Seth Meyers in early March. Asked whether he was ready for fatherhood, the former Nickelodeon star quipped, “I am terrified. I don’t know what to do with babies.” He also joked that he planned to be “at the casino” when Evangeline gave birth.
