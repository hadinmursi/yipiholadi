Reese Witherspoon to Produce Gone Girl Adaptation

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, August 15, 2024
WASHINGTON, DC - APRIL 28: Actress Reese Witherspoon attends the 98th Annual White House Correspondents’ Association Dinner at the Washington Hilton on April 28, 2012 in Washington, DC. (Photo by Stephen Lovekin/Getty Images)

Witherspoon, Bruna Papandrea, and Leslie Dixon will head up the adaptation of Gillian Flynn’s highly stressful thriller, Gone Girl, which is currently at the top of the New York Times “Best Seller List.” The rights reportedly went for seven figures; Flynn is attached to write the screenplay. Everyone stockpile your Xanax, this should be a doozy.
