Satoshi Kojima: My Elbow Drop Hit CM Punks Cock, But It Was Not On Purpose

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, September 2, 2024

Satoshi Kojima says he didn’t mean to hit CM Punk in the cock.

At AEW x NJPW Forbidden Door, Kojima faced CM Punk in an Owen Hart Foundation Cup Tournament match. The two veterans battled it out, and in one highlight, Kojima soared off the top rope with an elbow drop. The impact landed fairly low, as Punk grabbed his groin region, and it took him a few extra seconds to recover.

In a tweet, Kojima said he was sorry that his elbow drop hit Punk’s cock. He wrote that it was not on purpose, and it was an accident because he doesn’t have that kind of technology.

Kojima and AEW CEO Tony Khan exchanged kind words on social media, as the NJPW/AJPW star expressed his gratitude, and Khan said he would be a perfect fit for the tournament. He told Kojima it was an awesome match.
