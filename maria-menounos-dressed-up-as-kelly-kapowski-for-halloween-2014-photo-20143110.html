Maria Menounos Dressed Up as Kelly Kapowski for Halloween 2014: Photo

Posted by Patria Henriques on Friday, August 23, 2024

Calling all Bayside High students!

If you thought Justin Bieber paid the best tribute to Saved by the Bell‘s Kelly Kapowski with his throwback T-shirt in 2011, just wait: Maria Menounos arrived on the Live From E! set on Wednesday, Oct. 29, fully decked out as our favorite head cheerleader!

The TV host, 36, channeled Tiffani Thiessen‘s gorgeous good girl in paisley pants, a varsity jacket, and a crop top (from back when they first were a thing!). She also teased up her hair, ’80s-style, and added metallic eye shadow, making for one very cute (and only slightly early) #TBT.

And she wasn’t the only Bayside representative on set! Menounos was joined by her E! costars—Jason Kennedy played Zack Morris, complete with giant cell phone, and Ross Mathews rocked mixed prints as the series’ lovable nerd, Screech.

But do the E! anchors get your vote for best cast costumes? On Halloween day, Today‘s cast dressed up as classic Saturday Night Live characters, while the Good Morning America reporters did a mix of celebrities, Disney characters, and royals. And you can’t forget Kelly Ripa and Michael Strahan, who changed from zombies to Kimye to the lord and ladies of Downton Abbey, all in under an hour!
