Couple Names Child After Cody Rhodes

A couple out Scotland may want to change “Dat’s hard times, daddy!” to “Dat’s good times, baby!”  Paddy McPake and Kimberly Rogerson gave their newborn child the name of Cody after being an avid watcher of AEW Wrestling.

“Paddy, a keen wrestling fan had been watching a wrestling programme on television when he heard the name of former WWE wrestler Cody Rhodes, who goes under the moniker of ‘The American Nightmare’, and who previously wrestled under the name Stardust.

“Kimberley added: ‘Paddy likes his wrestling and when he heard it he said to me and I really liked it as well so he’s Cody.’
