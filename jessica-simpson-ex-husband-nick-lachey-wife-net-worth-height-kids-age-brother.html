Jessica Simpson Ex-Husband: Nick Lachey Wife, Net Worth, Height, Kids, Age, Brother

Posted by Tandra Barner on Thursday, August 29, 2024

Jessica Simpson is an American singer, fashion designer, actress and author who has been active in the media scenes since 1993.

Jessica Simpson played roles in the films titled The Master of Disguise, The Dukes of Hazzard, Employee of the Month, Blonde Ambition, The Love Guru, among others.

Jessica Simpson was engaged to Nick Lachey in February 2002 and they walked down the aisle on October 26 that year. Unfortunately, Jessica and Nick divorced in 2006.

Nick Lachey, known officially as Nicholas Scott Lachey, is an American singer and television personality who became famous as the lead singer of the 98 Degrees band. Nick Lachey won a number of accolades and notable among them include the Teen Choice Awards and ASCAP Pop Music Award.
