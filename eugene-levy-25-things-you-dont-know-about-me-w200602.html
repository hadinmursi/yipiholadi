Eugene Levy: 25 Things You Dont Know About Me

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Funnyman Eugene Levy, whose Pop TV show Schitt’s Creek airs at 8 p.m. on Wednesdays, shared 25 interesting things about himself with Us Weekly.

1. I truly admire what Donald Trump has done with his hair.

2. I am not of Nordic stock.

3. At 5-foot-10, I’m much taller than people think.

4. I have well over 7,000 close friends.

5. My pet scorpion is named Stingy.

6. I could be a romantic acting lead in Turkey.

7. Since Best in Show, I like to wear two left shoes.

8. I don’t think traveling is all it’s cracked up to be.

9. I recently experienced Beatlemania. Fun!

10. I have never lost a game of poker. Never. Not even once. I mean it.

11. I was Us Weekly’s Sexiest Man of the Year.

12. I lied.

13. My eyebrows could take Martin Scorsese’s.

14. I have never been on a best-dressed list.

15. I could have had a pop music career.

16. I was a Peter, Paul and Mary fan.

17. I’m considered a man of science.

18. I had a summer job as a garbage man.

19. I was originally cast as Forrest Gump. (OK, that was a lie.)

20. Playing the accordion did not make me a chick magnet.

21. I pass a kidney stone every 28 years.

22. I did not influence the wardrobe for Jim’s dad in the American Pie movies.

23. I hate raisins.
