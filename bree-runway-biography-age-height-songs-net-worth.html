Early Life

Bree Runway was born in November 18, 1992 in Hackney, London, where she also grew up.

Career

She began having interest in music at a very young age. She enrolled in Music technology subject where she learned the basics of music production. She began writing her own songs and doing beats beats with her DIY home studio.

She came through with her self released EPs, RNWY 01 and Bouji in November 2015 and May 2016 respectively. She released her debut single in November 2016 which was accompanied with a music video as shot in Dubai by model Leomie Anderson and directed by Runway.

She released another single “What do i tell my friends?” In September 2017 and in February 2017, she got featured on British electronic duo Metroplane’s single “Word of Mouth”.

Bree Runway eventually got signed in 2018 to Virgin EMI Records and released her label debit single “2ON” in May 2019 and in July the same year, she released “Big Racks” featuring Broke Candy

She was featured on the remix of Rina Sawayama’s single “XS” in July 2020 and later released singles such as “Gucci” alongside Maliibu Miitch in the same July and “Little Nokia” in September 2020.

She released her debut mix-tape, 2000and4eva under the imprint Virgin EMI records on November 6, 2020 and in 2021, she dropped lots of singles like “Hot Hot” and “Space Ghost Coast to Coast” featuring English rock band Glass Animals.

Awards & Nominations

Runway has got numerous nominations such as; Sound of 2021 at BBC awards and Video of the year at MOBO awards.
