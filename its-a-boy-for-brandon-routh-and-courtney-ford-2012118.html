It's a Boy for Brandon Routh and Courtney Ford!

Posted by Billy Koelling on Sunday, September 1, 2024

Congratulations are in order for Brandon Routh, star of the new CBS comedy Partners!

The actor, 32, and his wife Courtney Ford, 34, welcomed their first son, Leo James, on August 10, a rep for the couple confirms to Us Weekly. The newborn weighed 8 lbs. and measured 20.5 inches. James is also Brandon's middle name.

Ford, best known for her roles on True Blood, Dexter and Parenthood, married Routh in November 27 at the 3,000-acre Santa Barbara-area El Capitan Ranch of movie producers Jon and Mindy Peters. Guests included Routh's Superman Returns costar Kate Bosworth and director Bryan Singer.
