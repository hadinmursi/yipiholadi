Stephen Moyer News - Us Weekly

Posted by Billy Koelling on Thursday, August 22, 2024

Name:Stephen Moyer

Hometown:Brentwood, UK

Birthday:October 11, 1969

About

Making his film debut in 1997 as the lead role in Prince Valiant, which also starred Katherine Heigl, Stephen Moyer has always made his on-screen presence known. Born in 1969, the English native began his acting career in local theater.

His television break came in 2007 on USA’s The Starter Wife, starring alongside Debra Messing.

Jumping on the vampire bandwagon, Moyer joined the cast of True Blood in 2008, starring as Bill Compton–the sensitive and brooding vampire, who falls in love with Sookie Stackhouse, played by Anna Paquin.

Moyer has two children from previous relationships: Billy, born in 2000, and Lilac, born in 2002. He also has twins, Charlie and Poppy, with wife Paquin.
