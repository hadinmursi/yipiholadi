Ray Comfort Biography

Ray Comfort is a New Zealand-American Christian Minister and Evangelist, Author and Television Host who was born in Christchurch, New Zealand and brought-up in the United States. He is widely known as the Founder, President and CEO of Living Waters Publications and The Way of the Master based in Bellflower, California.

Comfort has also written numerous books including the 2009 book ‘You Can Lead an Atheist to Evidence, But You Can’t Make Him Think’, which was ranked #1 in Amazon.com’s atheism and apologetics categories. He has written and produced “180: Changing the Heart of a Nation”, a 33-minute documentary film.
