L.A. Rams' Wives Bond in Hollywood &amp; Football's First Promo

Posted by Kelle Repass on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

Grabbing reality TV by the horns! The Los Angeles Rams have returned to Southern California, and viewers get a glimpse at the players’ personal lives in Hollywood & Football, as seen in the first trailer above, which is exclusive to Us Weekly. 

Us can exclusively announce that the E! unscripted series launches Wednesday, November 30. It focuses on six Rams stars joining their wives and fiancées in adjusting to life in La-La Land, as the team has returned to its former stomping ground after more than 20 years in St. Louis. 

Participating in the series are wide receiver Kenny Britt, wife Sabrina and their two kids; offensive lineman Rodger Saffold, wife Asia and their two little ones; tight end Lance Kendricks and wife Danielle, who recently tied the knot; running back Chase Reynolds, wife Kila and their three children; wide receiver Bradley Marquez and pregnant fiancée Melissa; and fullback Cory Harkey, wife Bianca and their newborn.

“Not only are we a team, but we really are a family — we’re the ‘Ram-ily,'” says one of the players’ spouses in the promo.

The series’ premiere episode features Rodger and Asia throwing a summer bash for the team, although some surprise guests ruin the mood.

The Rams — who were also this season’s subjects of HBO’s acclaimed summer series Hard Knocks — have gotten off to a solid start so far in 2016. They currently hold a record of 3-2, putting them just a half-game back on the division-leading Seattle Seahawks.
