Joe Alwyns Brothers Ex Breaks Silence On Taylor Swifts New Album As He Fears How Itll Hu

Posted by Tandra Barner on Saturday, August 31, 2024

Highlights

  • Joe Alwyn's silence on Taylor Swift's upcoming album fuels speculation about their 2023 breakup among eager fans.
  • Brynn Cartelli's cryptic TikTok videos hint at a positive relation between a member of the Alwyn family and Taylor Swift.
  • Reports suggest Joe Alwyn is nervous about how Taylor's album will portray him, raising concerns about fan backlash.

Joe Alwyn has been infamously silent about Taylor Swift’s upcoming as well as their 2023 break-up, in general, though fans are still eager to know what happened between the couple after six years. With The Tortured Poet Department’s release scheduled for a few days away, many Swifties expect Taylor to shed light on her break-up with Joe, or at least provide some telling lyrics that’ll provide context.

Though Joe still hasn’t made any statement, someone close to him and his family has given her two thoughts on Taylor’s new music – his brother’s girlfriend, Brynn Cartelli. She expressed excitement over Tay’s album, admitting she’s counting down the days until its release, suggesting not all of the Alwyn family is on bad terms with the pop star after her and Joe’s split.

The Alwyn Family May Not Be Upset With Taylor

Cartelli, 20, sparked intrigue among social media users after sharing a TikTok video on Thursday, April 11, where she was seen eating with the caption, "Deeply upset with myself for lurking and thinking that I wouldn't hurt my own feelings."

Following up with another video, Cartelli captioned it, "Anyway, so excited for April 19," a clear reference to the release date for Taylor’s album.

Interestingly, both videos featured her in the same outfit, eating from the same bowl, and positioned at the same angle. While the second clip has been deleted from her account, it was subsequently reposted elsewhere, including X (formerly known as Twitter) causing speculation and curiosity among her followers.

While Brynn’s videos were vague and perhaps meant to stir up speculation as to their meaning, some social media users speculate that she has a side of the story to share regarding Taylor and Joe’s break-up. One person tweeted, “Oh these brothers are having a flop-off,” while another person wrote, “She definitely has some things to say.”

How Joe Reportedly Feels About Taylor’s Upcoming Album

There have been various reports claiming Joe is nervous about how Taylor’s album will portray him and whether it will send her dedicated fan base after him more so than they’ve already been, even though Taylor is now in a public relationship with Kansas Chiefs player Travis Kelce.

“It’s safe to say that Joe is bracing himself for a lot of questions. No one is safe,” a source previously told Life & Style. “Joe should be worried about what she reveals.”

During their relationship, it was speculated that Alwyn wasn’t as comfortable with the spotlight as Swift, given that they kept their romance largely private, though she’d been more open about her relationships in the past (as well as the present with Kelce). Taylor also has a reputation about opening up about her love life in her songs, so fans are confident she’ll do the same on her upcoming record.
