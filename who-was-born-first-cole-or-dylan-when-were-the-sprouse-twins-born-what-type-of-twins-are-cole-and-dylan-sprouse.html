Posted by Tandra Barner on Monday, August 26, 2024

Cole Sprouse was born 15 minutes after his twin brother Dylan Sprouse. The Sprouse Twins were born in Arezzo, Italy on August 4, 1992.

Dylan and Cole Sprouse are identical twins. Cole Sprouse is an American actor and photographer best known for his role as Cody Martin on the Disney Channel series The Suite Life of Zack & Cody and its spin-off series The Suite Life on Deck.

Some notable roles, Cole Sprouse shared with his brother include Patrick Kelly in the sitcom Grace Under Fire from 1993 to 1998, Julian in the 1999 film, Big Daddy, and young Pistachio Disguisey in 2002’s The Master of Disguise.

Cole Sprouse and his brother’s first role as separate characters in the same production was as kids in a MADtv sketch.

Cole Sprouse portrayed Cody Martin in the 2005 Disney Channel original series, The Suite Life of Zack & Cody alongside his brother. Cole Sprouse is a recipient of several accolades.
