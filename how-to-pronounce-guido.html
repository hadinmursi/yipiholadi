How do you say the name Guido?

G pronounced as in the word “go.” This is the Spanish pronunciation and is currently much more common than the Italian.

What is the name Guido in English?

Guido (plural Guidos) A male given name from Italian, equivalent to English Guy.

How do you say Guido in cars?

Guido also says OK in Cars. In Cars: The Video Game, Guido says boss in English. He also says “pit stop” in English, but pronounces it as “peet stop”. In the Italian version of the first film, he is voiced by Alex Zanardi.
