Cindy Crawford's Grandma, 97, Proves Her Family Is Genetically Blessed

Posted by Kelle Repass on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

It’s almost unfair! Cindy Crawford came from a long line of good genes, if the picture she posted to Instagram on September 16 is any indication.

While in her home state of Illinois, the supermodel, 50, visited her 97-year-old grandmother, who is just as photogenic as the ’90s legend. “Loved seeing one of my grandmas, Dorothy Walker in Chicago,” Crawford captioned the smiley pic of the two. “Still beautiful and feisty at 97!”

Of course the Becoming author, who is married to Casamigos Tequila cofounder Rande Gerber, has passed down her genetic good fortune as well. Both her son, Presley, 17, and daughter, Kaia, 15, have followed in her modeling footsteps.

Presley recently posed for CR Fashion Book and walked the runway for Moschino in June 2016, while Kaia has been featured in spreads for Teen Vogue and Love, as well as booking luxe brands like Miu Miu.
